Programme Overview

Career prospects of graduates from this MBioInno programme are promising and diverse, offering a wide range of opportunities for individuals interested in combining their passion for biomedical sciences, healthcare with a focus on innovation and technology.

Graduates will be equipped with practical and transferable skills applicable to a wide range of areas in both the public and private sectors, including established and start-up biotechnology and digital health companies, AI and digital health and telemedicine organisations, health data analysis and management centres, hospitals and clinics, investment banks, insurance companies, consultancy companies, STEM education, research centres, and government and private laboratories.

Capstone Experience and Industry Internship

The capstone project or industry internship provides students with valuable practical experience to advance their career in the industry or further studies.

Student should choose one option where the project should be designated as Capstone (CMB) or Capstone (DHT). For internship, relevance of the internship to a stream must be confirmed by the programme co-directors prior to the start of the internship.

Students will have a choice to conduct a capstone project with a HKUMed academic staff, or to conduct industry internship with one of our industry partners (this option is only available to full-time students).
