Featured Speakers

Professor Xuhua He
Member, The Hong Kong Academy of Sciences

Chair Professor in the Department of Mathematics at HKU. Distinguished mathematician in algebraic groups, representation theory, and arithmetic geometry. Recipient of the Morningside Gold Medal of Mathematics (2013), the Xplorer Prize (2020), and theamS Chevalley Prize in Lie Theory (2022). Elected Fellow of the Hong Kong Academy of Sciences in 2025.

Professor David Clifton
University of Oxford

Royal Academy of Engineering Chair of Clinical Machine Learning at the University of Oxford. Leads the Computational Health Informatics (CHI) Lab focusing on AI for Healthcare. NIHR Research Professor and first non-medical scientist to hold this flagship chair. Research focuses on advancing AI-based methods for healthcare with over 40 awards.

Professor Liz Jackson
The University of Hong Kong

Karen Lo Eugene Chuang Professor in Diversity and Equity and Associate Dean (Research) at the Faculty of Education, HKU. Research examines the sociology and philosophy of education with emphasis on diversity and social justice. President of the Comparative Education Society of Hong Kong and Editor‑in‑Chief of Educational Philosophy and Theory.

Dr. De Ming Chau
Universiti Putra Malaysia

Senior Lecturer in the Department of Biomedical Sciences. Governing Board Member of the World Conferences on Research Integrity Foundation. Over a decade of experience leading research integrity workshops. Member of the National Committee on Research Integrity, Malaysia. Co-founder of the Young Scientists Network-Academy of Sciences Malaysia RCR Programme.

Dr. Trevor Lane
The University of Hong Kong

Research Development and Impact Specialist at the Faculty of Dentistry, HKU. Trustee of the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE). Extensive experience in scholarly publishing and research communication. Co-author of the Good Publication Practice (GPP) Guidelines for Company-Sponsored Biomedical Research: 2022 update.

Professor Simon Young
The University of Hong Kong

Ian Davies Professor in Ethics at the Faculty of Law, HKU. Deputy Director of Education and Development for Research Integrity at HKU. Scholar-practitioner researching Hong Kong's constitutional order, human rights, and anti-money laundering laws. Editor-in-Chief of the Asia-Pacific Journal on Human Rights and the Law.

Professor Joshua Ho
The University of Hong Kong

Associate Professor and Assistant Dean (Innovation & Technology Transfer) at the LKS Faculty of Medicine, HKU. Specializes in computational analysis of single-cell, metagenomic, and digital health data. Featured speaker at HKU's Responsible Conduct of Research Seminars. Contributor to the development of Guidelines on the Responsible Use of Generative AI in Research at HKU.
